About Bread Day

Bread Day started as a small family business in 2015. Our founder, a passionate home baker, decided to turn her love for baking into a cozy bakery where the whole community could enjoy fresh, homemade bread every day.

Over the years, Bread Day has grown from a tiny kitchen into a full bakery shop, but we have never forgotten our roots. We still bake everything by hand, using simple and honest ingredients like flour, butter, eggs, and sugar.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring warm, fresh, and tasty baked goods to our customers every single day. We believe that good bread can bring people together, and we are proud to be a part of our local community.

Our Team

Our team is made up of friendly bakers and staff who love what they do. From early morning baking to friendly service at the counter, every member of our team works hard to make sure you have a great experience at Bread Day.
