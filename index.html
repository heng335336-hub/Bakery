Welcome to Bread Day!

Bread Day is a small neighborhood bakery that has been serving fresh, warm, and delicious baked goods since 2015. Every morning we bake our bread, cakes, cookies, and pastries fresh, using simple ingredients and traditional recipes.

If you are looking for a soft loaf of bread for your breakfast, a sweet cake for a birthday celebration, or a quick cookie for an afternoon snack, Bread Day has something for you.
