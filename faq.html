Frequently Asked Questions

1. What time does Bread Day open?

We open every day at 7:00 AM and close at 8:00 PM, including weekends.

2. Do you take custom cake orders?

Yes! We accept custom cake orders for birthdays, weddings, and other special occasions. Please contact us at least 2 days in advance.

3. Do you offer gluten-free or sugar-free products?

At this time, we do not offer gluten-free or sugar-free products, but we hope to add these options in the future.

4. Can I order online?

Currently, we do not have an online ordering system. Please visit our store or call us directly to place an order.

5. Do you offer delivery?

We offer local delivery for large orders such as birthday cakes. Please contact us for more details.
